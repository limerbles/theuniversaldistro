About Us

The Universal Distro is a licensed distribution company based out of West Hollywood in California with a premier branding and marketing team operating from Los Angeles. We have partnered with numerous cultivators, dispensaries, manufacturers, and other cannabis operators to create a full vertically integrated eco-system. Our vision is to breathe life back into this new and struggling industry by partnering with small and medium sized operators as well as potential social equity operators. We feel it is important to include these operators in the industry as they were the ones who cultivated the industry since 1996 as opposed to the new wave of venture capitalist whose primary interest is in financial gain. We currently service all of Southern California and we are looking to expand into Northern California.
