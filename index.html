About Us

The Universal Distro is a legal licensed cannabis distribution company based out of Los Angeles, California. We have a strong branding and marketing team operating out of Studio City and we have partnered with numerous cultivators, dispensaries, manufacturers, and other cannabis operators to create a full vertically integrated eco-system. Our vision is to breathe life back into this new and struggling industry by partnering with small and medium sized operators as well as potential social equity operators. We feel it is important to include these types of operators in the industry as they were the ones who cultivated the industry since 1996 as opposed to the new wave of venture capitalists whose primary interest is in financial gain. We currently service all of Southern California and we are looking to expand into Northern California. If you're interested in learning more or would like to work with us, please contact us.
